The Product

Our Broilers

Thirty-five-day mature broilers raised in clean, professionally managed housing—with nutrition, hydration, and daily care held to a standard you can see.

This is how we keep our birds: spacious, well-ventilated housing, dry litter underfoot, and modern feeding and drinking systems so every flock eats and drinks on demand—not in bursts when someone remembers.

Our facilities blend thoughtful floor-reared environments with disciplined, high-capacity systems. Quality feed is always within reach; water lines and drinkers stay reliable. Light, airflow, and housekeeping are part of the routine—not an afterthought—so the birds stay vigorous and the meat stays consistent.

At thirty-five days you get broilers at the right weight for kitchens and resale: firm, well-finished, and raised under conditions we are proud to show—because premium supply starts with how the birds are kept.

  • 35 days Mature, market-ready broilers
  • 24/7 access Feed & fresh water systems

Immaculate housing
